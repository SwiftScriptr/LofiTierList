Welcome to a taste of what Lofi is like

If you have just read the README and are intrigued to venture in this space with little effort, then this list is for you.
All Mini EP's here are great examples of what Lofi has to offer, whilst simultaneously being an appetizer of more to come.
EP's here seem to be in the Introduction category which is why I specifically decided under 12 Minutes, 4-5 tracks each EP max.

  1. Strehlow - Sleep in Love

    2. This is a wide range of intimate funk that will have you getting the goosies (in the best way).
    The track revolves around RnB, funk and rythmic Jazz.

  2. SwØrn - Escapade

    3. A more upbeat track than the first, is more dynamic but I am unsure which is better interms of uniqueness and style.

  3. Parkbench Epiphany - Antimidas

    4. Lofi's secret weapon is the ability to soothe and destress, if you want to feel and confront your emotions, this EP is a way of doing so.
    I advise not to do anything while thought intensive while listening to this to help lower cortisol levels.

  4. Miscél - The other Side

    5. This exposes you to the ability to create a world through music, like how some sounds are associated to comon blockbuster genres like sci-fi and so forth. Therefore a little bit more in deph of what stories Lofi can tell through little to no vocals.

  5. Psalm Trees X Guilliume Muschalle - Céleste

    6. Needed for a variety of music that is chilled but effectively beutifull at the same time, giving you a healthy dopamine hit.

Well that's all for our intro EP's into Lofi, I hope you leave your thoughts below!
