The masters of lofi albums

Here will be a shortlist of curators I reccomend to check out due to their consistent passion of their graft.
These artists either have a multitude of talents or do the best unique take on musical story-telling that seperates them from others;
simply put, their consistency isn't a 'Brand' of music but rather they creatively pop out Quality music establishing their own preferences and flare.
These guys therefore have pumped out enough content over the last decade (and a half to those OG social media composers on YT) Please note that the top 5 reccomendations are in no particular order, except from one french dude which I respect the most for his talents;
those outside the five will have joint positions in the 6th position respectively.
